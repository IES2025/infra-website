About Faumil

Faumil LDA is a well-established company based in Mozambique, specializing in manufacturing, procurement, and supply of materials for various industries, including defense and military sectors. As a key provider for the Mozambican Army, Faumil ensures high-quality production standards and efficient logistics to meet critical supply needs.

With a strong supply chain network and extensive expertise in material sourcing, Faumil LDA plays a crucial role in enhancing industrial and military operations by delivering top-grade resources and equipment to its clients.
